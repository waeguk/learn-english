📝 Today's English Quiz: Daily Life & Common Sense

1. Why do people wear seat belts in a car?
자동차에서 사람들은 왜 안전벨트를 착용할까요?




2. What should you do if your phone battery is almost dead?
휴대폰 배터리가 거의 방전되었을 때 무엇을 해야 할까요?




3. Which activity is generally considered healthy?

4. If it starts raining heavily, what should you take with you?

5. Why do people drink water?
사람들은 왜 물을 마실까요?




6. What is the opposite of "expensive"?

7. If someone says, "Could you repeat that?"
누군가 "Could you repeat that?"이라고 말하면 무슨 뜻일까요?




8. Which food is usually sweet?
일반적으로 어떤 음식이 달콤할까요?




9. What should you do before crossing a road?
길을 건너기 전에 무엇을 해야 할까요?




10. Why is learning English useful?
